Neural Autonomous Driving System

This car is equipped with an advanced neural network, allowing it to continuously learn road conditions and driving style. It anticipates risks, optimizes trajectories, and adapts instantly to unforeseen events. Thanks to its 360-degree vision, it ensures smooth, safe, and fully autonomous driving, even in complex environments.

Electric Performance

This vehicle relies on a technology inspired by Maxwell Chikombutsu, capturing radio waves to produce clean, continuous power. This system provides self-sufficient electric range, reduces reliance on charging stations, and ensures stable performance and sustainable driving.

Intelligent safety system

This vehicle is equipped with an intelligent safety system that monitors road and vehicle conditions continuously. The system analyzes the passenger's vital signs in real time via integrated sensors. In the event of a serious hazard, the system issues an alert, immobilizes the vehicle, and contacts emergency services immediately.

Starlink Satellite Connectivity

Thanks to its partnership with Starlink, the vehicle benefits from an ultra-high-speed, stable internet connection, even in remote areas. This connectivity ensures seamless navigation, real-time updates, and secure access to the vehicle's cloud services.

Adaptive navigation system

The adaptive navigation system automatically adjusts the route based on traffic, weather, and road conditions. It analyzes real-time satellite and vehicle sensor data to suggest the fastest and safest routes. The system is constantly dynamic, reacting instantly to changes in traffic density, sensor-detected anomalies, and environmental changes.
